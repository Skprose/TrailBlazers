Resources and Tips for Trail Expeditions


  • Take out ONLY what you took in, take out ALL that you took in

  • Respect the wildlife and environs by not feeding the creatures - It's safe for you and safe for them

  • Stay on designated, marked paths

  • Take sunscreen, hats, natural and eco-friendly repellents, and plenty of water

  • Take pictures, but don't take the flowers, rocks, trees, gravel, etc.

  • Let someone know your destination, and your time of return

  • Keep in accordance to all trail/park guidelines and hours

  • Remember that noise pollution is also pollution - Respect your fellow adventurers and Shhhhhh, please

  • Live, Learn, and Love the Great Outdoors!
